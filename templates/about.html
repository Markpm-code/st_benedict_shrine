Our Story beginnings

St.Benedict's Garden of life is a 2.2 acres of land enshrouded in forest greenery at the slopes of the lofty Mt. Malindang in Mindanao providing a majestic view of the sorrrounding towns. It is located in the town of Bonifacio in the village of Sito Migsali, Misamis Occidental, Philippines

It was founded by Fr. Vicente 'Inting' Mandaya in the year 1998. It took him years to develop the shrine and faces loads of challenges along the way. With his resiliency and his dedication to serve God and the people in his ministry, He withstood them all and continued developing the St.Benedict's Garden of life.


The background of the image above where Fr. Inting is pointing at is where the St. Benedict is being develop in the early years when it was founded.
